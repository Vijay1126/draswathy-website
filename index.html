Experience

Trusted pediatric care for over 25 years

With over 25 years of dedicated experience in pediatrics, Dr. Aswathy Pavithran has built her career on trust, care, and excellence.

She began her medical journey at TD Medical College, Alappuzha, followed by a postgraduate specialization at KMC, Manipal. Over the years, she has served as a Consultant Pediatrician at some of Kerala’s leading institutions, including Dr. Nair’s Hospital, Kollam, Sankers Hospital, Kollam, and City Hospital, Ernakulam, where she consulted until 2025.

Today, she continues her commitment to child health at Vijayalakshmi Medical Centre, Kochi, where her wealth of knowledge and compassionate approach make her a trusted name among families.
